How to use these papers

  • Section A (12 marks, ≈15 mins): choose EITHER 1(a) OR 1(b) — an "examine the similarities/differences" question. Structure as paired points: a similarity/difference, explained, with an example for each side.
  • Section B (12 marks, ≈15 mins): compulsory — "analyse the differences" between two theoretical views, explicitly naming the core political ideas/thinkers involved.
  • Section C (2 × 30 marks, ≈45 mins each): pick TWO of the three "evaluate the view..." essays. Always argue both sides in a genuinely balanced way, make synoptic links across Global Politics topics, and use a contemporary example for each point.
  • Total timing: 2 hours for 84 marks — roughly 1 minute per mark, so allow about 15+15+45+45 minutes across the four questions, with 5 minutes to spare for checking.
